# 🛒 E-Commerce Business Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce transactional data using **SQL, Python, and Power BI** to uncover insights into customer behavior, product performance, sales trends, and inventory management.

The objective is to transform raw business data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making across marketing, sales, and operations.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Addressed

### Customer Insights

* Which cities have the highest concentration of customers?
* What is the distribution of customer order frequencies?
* How effective is customer acquisition over time?

### Product Analysis

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* Which products are commonly purchased together?
* Which product categories have the broadest customer reach?
* Which products drive repeat purchases?

### Sales Analysis

* What are the month-over-month sales growth trends?
* How does Average Order Value (AOV) change over time?
* Which months experience peak demand?

### Inventory Management

* Which products have the highest turnover rates?
* Which products are at risk of becoming dead stock?

## 📂 Dataset Information

The analysis uses four datasets:

| Dataset      | Description                       |
| ------------ | --------------------------------- |
| Customers    | Customer details and locations    |
| Products     | Product catalog and categories    |
| Orders       | Customer orders and sales amounts |
| OrderDetails | Product-level transaction details |

### Dataset Size

* Customers: 100 Records
* Products: 8 Records
* Orders: 200 Records
* OrderDetails: 200 Records

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Customer Insights

* Delhi and Chennai contain the largest customer bases.
* Most customers place only 1–2 orders.
* New customer acquisition showed a declining trend after mid-2023.

### Product Insights

* Laptop 15 Pro generated the highest revenue.
* Digital SLR Camera was the second-highest revenue contributor.
* Electronics emerged as the most popular product category.

### Sales Insights

* Sales peaked during September and December.
* Significant month-to-month fluctuations indicate seasonal demand.
* Average Order Value increased during high-sales periods.

### Inventory Insights

* Digital SLR Camera exhibited the highest turnover rate.
* Smartphones and Wireless Earbuds showed relatively lower customer reach.
* Several products demonstrated strong repeat-purchase behavior.
